Kommuna Kollab Vision

Kommuna Kollab was built to empower student creators in Manila — musicians, filmmakers, artists, designers, and performers — to rise together instead of struggling alone.

We envision a united community where students can access equipment, studios, collaboration spaces, gigs, and opportunities regardless of financial limitations.

By providing tools, support, and a safe place to grow, we aim to build a generation of creators who uplift each other and shape the local arts scene with purpose.

“Create with purpose. Collaborate with passion.”
